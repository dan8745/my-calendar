# <img src="https://raw.githack.com/FortAwesome/Font-Awesome/master/svgs/solid/calendar.svg" card_color="#22A7F0" width="50" height="50" style="vertical-align:bottom"/> My Calendar
Retrieves the days events from your calendar

## About
Connects to a caldav server, such as fruux or nextcloud, and lists the summary of events you have for the next 24 hours.

## Examples
* "What is on my calendar today"
* "What do i have going on today"
* "What events do i have today"
